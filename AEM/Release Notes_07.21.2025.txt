AEM Release Notes - July 21, 2025:
- Introduced new outputs for Thermal Diffusivity and Specific Heat Capacity in Report 5 and Deby Length in Report 11.
- Expanded compound library with new species for aqueous flow battery simulations.
- Option to use a low-concentration salt scale with 0.001 molal step size.
- Added support for dual ACCC salts within a single simulation.
- Added two new ACCC solvent input terms:
  - Pure-solvent melting temperature (°C)
  - Activation energy for melting behavior (kJ/mol)
  - These are used to estimate free liquid availability in systems near solid-liquid phase transitions, impacting viscosity and conductivity.
- Improvements in calculation time and temperature range for large-scale matrix optimization.
